Introduction to ADAL Smart Naga

Understanding the purpose and vision behind the AI-powered ordinance chatbot for Naga City

The Problem

Access to government information is a fundamental right of every citizen. However, in Naga City, like many local government units in the Philippines, ordinances and regulations are scattered across hundreds of PDF documents. This creates several challenges:

  • Difficult Discovery: Finding relevant ordinances requires knowing exact ordinance numbers or manually searching through multiple files
  • Time-Consuming: Reading through lengthy PDF documents to find specific information takes hours
  • Technical Barrier: Not everyone is comfortable navigating government websites or file systems
  • Language Complexity: Legal language in ordinances can be difficult to understand for average citizens
  • Limited Accessibility: PDFs are not mobile-friendly and don't support conversational queries
Real Impact: Citizens who need information about business permits, traffic regulations, or their rights often give up searching or rely on incomplete secondhand information.

The Solution

ADAL Smart Naga transforms government transparency through conversational AI. Instead of searching through PDFs, citizens can simply ask questions in plain English and receive instant, accurate answers with source citations.

How It Works

  1. User asks a question: "What are the penalties for noise violations in Naga City?"
  2. Hybrid search: The system combines semantic understanding (70%) with keyword matching (30%) to find relevant ordinance sections
  3. AI generation: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash generates a natural language answer based on retrieved ordinances
  4. Source attribution: Every answer includes clickable links to the original ordinance documents
  5. Conversation memory: Follow-up questions are understood in context for natural dialogue

Key Innovations

  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): Ensures answers are grounded in actual ordinance text, not hallucinated
  • Hybrid Search: Balances semantic understanding with exact keyword matching for optimal accuracy
  • OCR Processing: Handles both modern PDFs and scanned historical documents
  • Streaming Responses: Real-time answer generation for better user experience

Target Users

Primary Users: CSPC Community

Currently, ADAL Smart Naga is available exclusively to the Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges (CSPC) community via @cspc.edu.ph and @my.cspc.edu.ph email addresses.

  • Students: Research Naga City governance for academic projects, understand local regulations for community engagement
  • Faculty & Researchers: Quick access to ordinances for case studies, teaching resources for civics courses
  • Administrators: Reference material for institutional compliance with local regulations

Future Expansion: Public Access

The pilot program with CSPC will inform expansion to all Naga City residents and stakeholders:

  • Citizens: Understand rights, benefits, and obligations under city ordinances
  • Business Owners: Research permit requirements and compliance regulations
  • Government Employees: Quick reference tool for public inquiries and policy questions
  • Journalists & Advocates: Access to government documents for transparency work

Value Proposition

For Citizens

  • Save Time: Get answers in seconds instead of hours of PDF searching
  • Natural Language: Ask questions conversationally, no technical knowledge required
  • Verifiable Answers: Every response links to original ordinance documents
  • Mobile Friendly: Access from any device, anywhere

For Government

  • Transparency: Demonstrates commitment to open government and citizen engagement
  • Reduced Inquiries: Automated answers free up staff for complex citizen services
  • Usage Analytics: Understand which ordinances citizens care about most
  • Scalable: Can expand to other cities, provinces, or national agencies

For Developers

  • Open Source: MIT License allows community contributions and customization
  • Modern Stack: Flask, Qdrant, Gemini, Supabase - production-ready technologies
  • Well Documented: Complete setup guides, API references, and architecture docs
  • Easy Deployment: One-click deployment to Render, Railway, or other platforms

Real-World Use Cases

Example Queries

Scenario Query ADAL Response
Business Owner "What are the requirements for street vendor permits?" Lists registration requirements, fees, and penalties from relevant ordinances with document links
Senior Citizen "What benefits am I entitled to?" Explains senior citizen benefits, eligibility, and how to claim them
Student Researcher "Find all ordinances related to traffic management" Retrieves and summarizes multiple ordinances on traffic, parking, and road safety
Homeowner "What is Ordinance 2025-047 about?" Provides full summary with title, purpose, key provisions, and penalties
