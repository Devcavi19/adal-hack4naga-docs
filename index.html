About ADAL Smart Naga

ADAL (AI-Powered Academic Librarian) for Naga City is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot system that democratizes access to Naga City Government ordinances and regulations. Built by Team Virgo from CSPC BSCS-3B for the Naga City Hackathon.
